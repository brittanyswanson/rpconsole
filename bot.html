Commands

Commands are always prefaced with an exclamation mark (!).

species

Purpose: Shows the current available species

Input: None

Example: !species

char_list

Purpose: Shows the active characters of a species

Input: species
note: Ensure spelling and capitalization is exact

Example: !char_list VAMPIRES

app

Purpose: Shows the application url for a desired character

Input: "CHARACTER NAME"
notes:

  • Ensure spelling and capitalization is exact.
  • Don't skip the quotes!
  • There's a space between the command and your input.

Example: !app "MARTY MCFLY"
